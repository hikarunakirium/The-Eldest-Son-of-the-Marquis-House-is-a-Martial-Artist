"Haa…"

Dar, who arrived at the training ground before Yuren, sighed when he saw Veil's chilling eyes from the nobles' seating area.

Veil usually acted intellectual, but in reality he was a bloodthirsty murderer who could crush armor and swords with his bare hands and tear people apart alive.

Thinking that if things went wrong, that murderer's rage might explode on him, Dar swallowed dryly and turned his gaze away.

"Yuren Rox…"

Yuren was currently the hottest topic in the kingdom. He had slain multiple powerful monsters and gained such fame that Duke Roper personally called him the greatest contributor to the northern wall reclamation.

Yuren was a powerhouse shaking the kingdom, but Dar never once considered the possibility of his own defeat.

"I have Kovis Archery."

Dar had learned the ancient archery technique called Kovis Archery from the magic tome Veil gave him.

The mysterious abilities of Kovis Archery had turned the ordinary training knight Dar into the Shattering Archer Dar.

Moreover, after analyzing Yuren's fighting style from the information Veil provided, this was a fight Dar could not possibly lose.

'This guy…'

Grind.

Although he couldn't kill Yuren as the host said, Dar clenched his teeth, determined to at least cripple him so he couldn't move for a while.

"Begin!"

The moment the spar started, Yuren took four steps to the left and raised his finger. Dar noticed a thumbnail-sized metal bead held between Yuren's middle and thumb fingers.

'Not a dagger, but a bead? He thinks I can't hit small objects? What an idiot!'

Dar assumed Yuren brought beads instead of daggers as a counter to his arrows.

Dodging the bead and shooting would have been wiser, but Dar decided to show off his skill and aimed at the metal bead Yuren was holding.

Paaang!

Ping!

The instant Yuren flicked the bead, Dar also released the fully drawn bowstring.

Clang!

A tremendous impact sound that didn't seem like a collision between bead and arrow shook the training ground.

"Kuk."

Dar smirked at Yuren's pathetic idea and reached for another arrow from his quiver when he suddenly felt something right in front of his forehead. And then…

Bam!

He lost consciousness.
