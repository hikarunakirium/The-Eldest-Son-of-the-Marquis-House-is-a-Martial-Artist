Chapter 58: Lava Golem

"Huk… huk…"

Tesstein, the chief of the Red Hammer Clan, felt a bitter taste in his mouth for the first time in a long while.

The battle against the lava golems, which started even before the steel gate installed to block intruders was destroyed, had continued for over an hour.

"Damn rock lumps! Where the hell is the core?!"

"Deal with the one on the left first!"

"I want to, but the ground is covered in lava, I can't get close!"

"These things usually come one at a time. Why did three show up at once?!"

The dwarves gripped their blunt weapons tightly until their hands bled, determined not to retreat.

"Kuaaaa."

Bububub.

The lava golems dripped lava from their outer shells, slowly narrowing the space where the dwarves could move.

"Ugh…"

If it had been stone golems, they would have crushed them long ago, even if there were ten instead of three.

But lava golems have the characteristic of lava flowing from their shells. For dwarves who must fight in close quarters with physical attacks, they were the worst possible opponents.

Three lava golems attacking at the same time made it natural for the dwarves to be helplessly pushed back.

"Chief. If we get pushed here, the village will be destroyed."

"You have to decide now — either retreat or fight to the end here!"

"Of course we're not retreating. Even if we die, we end it here!"

"Understood!"

"Obviously!"

Tesstein and the dwarves gritted their teeth and glared at the golems that had advanced to the center of the village.

Everyone knew. The short moment when the drainage channel in the village center would drain the lava was the last chance to defeat them.

"Now! Everyone attack!"

"Uwaaaaah!"

"You rock lumps! I'll show you the ferocity of the Red Hammer Clan!"

The moment the dwarves heard Tesstein's command and charged at the golems—

Paaang!

A sound like air exploding came from somewhere, and the lava golem rampaging in the middle suddenly stopped moving.

Thud.

The lava golem's right arm, which had been frozen in place, fell off with a dull sound.

Kugugugugu.

Not just the right arm. The lava golem that had been going berserk just moments ago crumbled smoothly like sand.

"Huh?"

"W-what…"

"What the hell…"

The dwarves, watching the golem collapse for no apparent reason, stared in a daze at the steaming stones that had been lava golems just seconds ago.

"A… human?"

Tesstein, the first to regain his senses, saw a human man with a confident smile walking into the village from behind the collapsed golem.

"Greetings later. Let's put out the urgent fire first. Or rather, the lava."

After speaking, the man raised his hand. His hand was aimed at the lava golems on the right and left.

Paaang!

Paaang!

The moment something shot out from both of the man's hands, drawing white lines and piercing the golems—

The golems melted down.

"Huh?"

"The golem's core…"

The problem was finding the core. Once the core was found, catching a golem was as easy as lying down and eating rice cakes.

Tap.

Sssssss.

The lava golem whose core was destroyed stopped moving and crumbled like a sandcastle.

"Huh?"

The red-bearded dwarf with a dazed face looked at me. I smiled at him and then searched for the cores of the remaining two lava golems.

One had its core in the left chest, the other in the center of its abdomen.

Paaang!

Paaang!

I launched White Light Orbs with both hands and simultaneously destroyed the two lava golems.

Kugugugugu.

The moment I lowered my hands, the melting lava golems created an incredibly cool scene.

Sssss.
